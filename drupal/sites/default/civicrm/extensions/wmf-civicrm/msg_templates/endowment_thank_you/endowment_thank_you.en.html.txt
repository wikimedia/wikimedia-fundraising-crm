With this donation to Wikimedia’s long-term security fund, you're allowing us to look far ahead, with hope and confidence that free knowledge will be supported for generations to come.</p>

<p>I founded Wikipedia 20 years ago. Since then, our beloved encyclopedia has grown a lot, through good times and hardships, and today, more than ever, it is serving its true purpose. It helps millions of people study, discover, explore, and research.</p>

<p>Each day, Wikipedia gives readers a new chance to acquire the knowledge that is so rightfully theirs, no matter what their circumstances might be.</p>

<p>You’re the one who makes this possible. Your support allows edits to be made and articles to reach new readers; it gives our non-profit the means to stay true to its mission, even as Wikipedia has become one of the most visited websites in the world.</p>

{if $gift_source eq 'Retirement Fund - Endowment Specified' or  $gift_source eq 'Retirement Fund'}

This is why the Wikimedia Foundation directs all QCD donations to the Wikimedia Endowment, where they can continue to serve as long-term investments that will generate financial support for Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects in perpetuity.</p>
{/if}
